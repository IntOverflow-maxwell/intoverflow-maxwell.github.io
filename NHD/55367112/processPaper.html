PROCESS PAPER
The Marian reform is a major military reform that happened in the late Roman Republic, when it was rapidly expanding. Transforming from a regional power to the powerhouse of Mediterranean.

Our team are all history students who have interest in the Byzantium empire and Roman empire, when we saw the theme of turning points in history, we immediately dived into the thousand year history of Rome and Byzantium, trying to look for an event. We were struggling, until one day our history teacher mentioned that Rome only allowed citizens with properties to join the army in the beginning but later on allowed everyone to join in the Marian Reform. Our attentions jumped at this fact, we immediately dived into researching the Marian Reform, discovering this is a important reform that reshaped the conscription and the structure of the Roman army, which made it one of the most effective in the world at the time. The next day we went to ask the ancient history teacher Mister Lonergan about more information. After much researching and knowledge provided by our teachers, our team had decided on Marian Reform as the topic as it completely changed the Roman army allowing Rome to expand into an empire but also placing more personal loyalty among soldiers, which led to the beginning of the end of Roman democracy as power start to transition from Senate to Generals

After deciding on the topic, we went to research online to gather scholar opinion regarding Marian reform and how it had affected the capabilities of the army. In the process we also consulted Roman scholars that we have acquainted in our previous research programs to gather information regarding the background of the history regarding the army tactics and formations Roman army had employed when they fought against their enemies prior to the Marian reform.

After the research, we have decided on the website as the medium to present as some of us are quite acquainted with information technology, The website presented some unique challenges, adapting to a new development environment was hard and made a lot of things impossible, but also opened up a new window of possibility. It was a difficult task to design a website without using the JavaScript coding language, but we managed to learn how to do so within the time frame. There were also some challenges implementing the menu animations while keeping them clickable. We made progress eventually, and pushed through.

Rome was facing series of failure prior to Marian reform, but after the Marian reform they were able to rapidly expand, but so did the occasions of general trying to seize control, demonstrating the shifting of power in Rome. This event marks the demise of ancient democracies in the Mediterranean region and the rise of autocratic monarchs that will dominate most of the world for the next 2000 years.
